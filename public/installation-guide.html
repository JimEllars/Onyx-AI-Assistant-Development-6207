Onyx AI Installation Guide

Overview

Onyx AI Assistant is a powerful executive assistant designed specifically for James Ellars at AXiM Global. This guide will help you install both the main application on your Pixel phone and the companion app on your Google Watch.

Requirements: Google Pixel phone with Chrome browser, Google Watch with Wear OS 3+

Installing Onyx on Your Pixel Phone

  1. Open Chrome on your Pixel phone

    Launch the Chrome browser on your Pixel device.

  2. Navigate to the Onyx web app

    Visit https://onyx-ai.axim-global.com in your browser.

  3. Log in to your account

    Use the following credentials:

    • Email: demo@axim.global
    • Password: axim2023
  4. Install as a PWA

    You will see an "Install Onyx AI" banner at the bottom of the screen. Tap "Install" to add it to your home screen.

    Alternatively, tap the three dots menu in Chrome, then select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen".

  5. Complete the onboarding

    Follow the onboarding wizard to connect your Google Gemini API and Google Watch.

Installing Onyx on Your Google Watch

  1. Connect your watch to your phone

    Make sure your Google Watch is paired with your Pixel phone via the Wear OS app.

  2. Enable the Watch companion

    In the Onyx app on your phone, go to Settings → Google Watch Integration and tap "Connect Watch".

  3. Accept permissions

    On your watch, accept the permission requests for health data access and microphone usage.

  4. Install the watch app

    The companion app will automatically install on your watch when you connect it from the phone app.

Tip: For a quick preview of how the watch app works, tap the watch icon in the bottom right corner of the main Onyx app.

Troubleshooting

If the watch doesn't connect:

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
  • Make sure your watch is updated to the latest Wear OS version
  • Try restarting both your phone and watch
  • In the Wear OS app, check if the connection is stable

If Gemini integration fails:

  • Verify your API key is correct
  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure you have sufficient quota in your Google AI Studio account
